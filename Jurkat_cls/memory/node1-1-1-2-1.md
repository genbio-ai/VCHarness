- Test F1: 0.3953 vs parent 0.4572 (severe regression of -0.062)
- Val F1: peaked at 0.4572 (epoch 36), then oscillated without improvement for 20 epochs before early stopping
- Training duration: 56 epochs
- Train loss: monotonic decline 0.690→0.310 (overfitting)

**Key Issues**
- Excessive model capacity: ~5.1M-parameter output head (Linear 256→19920) alone is 74% of 6.9M total trainable params, causing memorization
- Overfitting: train_loss decreased monotonically while val_loss increased after epoch 26
- LoRA r=8 on all 8 layers (196K params) vs tree-best's r=4 contributed to overfitting without generalization benefit
