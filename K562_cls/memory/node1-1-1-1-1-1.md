**Improvements from Parent**
- Attention dimension reduced from 64→32 (attn_dim=32)
- PPI neighborhood expansion from K=16→32 neighbors per gene
- Weight decay increased from 3e-2→4e-2
- Cosine annealing schedule extended from T_max=100→150

**Results & Metrics (vs Parent)**
- Test F1=0.4743 (regression of -0.0103 from parent's 0.4846)
- Best checkpoint at epoch 51 (vs parent's epoch 70)
- Early stopping triggered at epoch 56 of 220 max_epochs (vs parent's epoch 70)
- Val F1 oscillated between 0.462–0.474 in late training
- Total parameters: ~5.38M (approximately equal to parent)

**Key Issues**
- K=32 expansion with halved attn_dim=32 created information bottleneck
- Degraded attention selectivity due to reduced per-neighbor representation capacity
- Shift from "selective high-dim attention" (K=16, attn_dim=64) to "broad noisy neighborhood" (K=32, attn_dim=32) was counterproductive despite similar parameter count
- Model converged to lower local optimum 19 epochs earlier than parent
