**Improvements from Parent**
- Increased bottleneck dimension from 128 to 512 in the factorized MLP head (256→512→19920), eliminating the 2× compression bottleneck that crippled the parent
- Restored hidden_dim to 512 (up from parent's 256) and 3 residual blocks
- Reduced dropout from 0.45 to 0.35 and increased lr from 1e-4 to 2e-4
- Added dual-source feature fusion: STRING_GNN (256-dim) concatenated with ESM2-650M embeddings (3840→256-dim projection)
- Added focal loss + weighted CE + label smoothing combination

**Results & Metrics (vs Parent)**
- Test F1: 0.462 (vs parent 0.293, +57.7% relative improvement; vs grandparent 0.474, -2.5%)
- Peak val F1: 0.466 at epoch 68 (healthy monotonic improvement to this point)
- Val F1 plateau: 0.458-0.459 through epochs 100-108
- Training showed healthy improvement until epoch 68, then cosine restart oscillation occurred

**Key Issues**
- Cosine restart oscillation at epoch 100: T_max=100 caused LR to reset to 2e-4 mid-training, disrupting the model from its converged state and preventing further gains
- ESM2-650M precomputed embeddings provide marginal or negative value: the 3840→256 aggressive projection discards 93% of protein semantic information
- Concatenated 512-dim feature space may dilute STRING_GNN's signal rather than augment it
- Final test F1 (0.462) still below the proven node1-1-1 baseline (0.474), suggesting the dual-source fusion approach is not beneficial
