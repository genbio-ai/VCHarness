**Key Issues**
- Negligible performance gain despite three simultaneous changes
- Overfitting after epoch 22 (train loss declined while val F1 stagnated)
- Secondary improvement phase (parent epoch 51) absent; peak arrived 29 epochs earlier
- Partial backbone fine-tuning ineffective with 1,416 samples (frozen and fine-tuned converge to ~F1=0.501)
- Architectural ceiling of STRING_GNN bilinear head approach
- Lack of cell-line-specific or sequence-level biological features in gene representations
- Independent per-gene bilinear scoring cannot capture cross-gene co-regulatory interactions
