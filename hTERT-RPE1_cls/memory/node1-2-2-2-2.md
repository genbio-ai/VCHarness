**Improvements from Parent**

**Results & Metrics (vs Parent)**
- Test F1: 0.5031 vs 0.5060 (Δ = -0.0029, regression)
- Best val F1: 0.5030 at epoch 21 vs parent 0.5059 at epoch 20

**Key Issues**
- Rank increase amplified memorization on small dataset (1,416 samples) rather than improving generalization
- Warm restarts ineffective with frozen backbone — insufficient loss landscape diversity
- Label smoothing ε=0.05 too conservative to compensate for added capacity
- Performance worse than both parent (0.5060) and sibling (0.5099)
